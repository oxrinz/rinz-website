# Distributed (federated) learning
<br />
There has been alot of talk about how centralized AI is dangerous to the society, and the different ways of preventing it from happening.
<br />
<br />
As of now, Facebooks Llama is our only hope of getting state of the art AI models out to the public. 
Obviously, we can't count on one company to save us all, we need a more reliable way of ensuring that centralized AI doesn't happen.
<br />
<br />
One of the recent hopes of essentially forcing big companies to open source their models is distributed, or federated learning.
